Excellence in Mathematics Education

Located in Canada, Palaris Maths School was founded in 2013 by a Master of Mathematics graduate from Wilfrid Laurier University with over 20 years of teaching experience. We provide both in-person and online classes for students from Grade 1 to Grade 12, creating a supportive and encouraging learning environment where every child can grow with confidence. At Palaris Maths School, we believe that every student has the ability to succeed in mathematics with the right guidance and consistent practice. Many students who joined us with a B-level standing have steadily improved to achieve A and even A+ results. Through personalized attention, structured learning, and strong foundational training, we help students overcome challenges and build lasting confidence. Over the years, our graduates have gone on to receive offers from respected universities such as Western University, the University of Waterloo, and the University of Toronto in fields including Computer Science, Medical Sciences, Business, and Engineering. Their success reflects not only academic achievement, but also the discipline and confidence developed throughout their journey with us.
